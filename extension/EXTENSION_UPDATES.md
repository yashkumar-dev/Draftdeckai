**The world's first AI-powered job application automation!**

### 2. 🎤 **10-Minute Voice Interview with AI Persona**

**Experience REAL interview simulation with voice interaction!**

#### Features:
- **🗣️ Real-Time Voice Conversation**: Speak naturally with AI interviewer
- **👤 AI Interviewer Persona**: "Alex" - professional interviewer voice
- **⏱️ 10-Minute Sessions**: Realistic time-boxed interviews
- **📊 Live Transcription**: See your answers in real-time
- **🎯 Dynamic Questions**: AI adapts based on your answers
- **💬 Follow-up Questions**: Just like real interviews
- **📈 Detailed Evaluation**: Get scored feedback after

#### Interview Flow:
1. **Introduction** - AI introduces itself professionally
2. **5-6 Questions** - Progressively challenging
3. **Follow-ups** - Based on your answers
4. **Real-time Feedback** - Natural acknowledgments
5. **Final Evaluation** - Comprehensive scoring

#### Evaluation Includes:
- Overall Score (0-100)
- Strengths (3-5 points)
- Areas for Improvement
- Communication Quality Rating
- Technical Accuracy Rating
- Final Recommendation (Strong Hire / Hire / Maybe / No Hire)

#### Voice Features:
- **Natural Speech**: Professional interviewer voice
- **Speech Recognition**: Understands your answers
- **Silence Detection**: Knows when you're done
- **Interruption Handling**: Can pause/resume
- **Time Management**: Tracks 10-minute limit
